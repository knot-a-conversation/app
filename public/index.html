K(NOT) A CONVERSATION

This is the companion app for the K(not) a Conversation installation exploring the concepts of artificial intelligence, creativity and labour. Inspired by ancient indigenous communication systems of Quipu knots, it instigates a communication and reflection process through the use of knots and AI systems.

Instructions

Move your viewfinder around the sculpture. Bring your camera closer to the knots and you will find some questions embedded in them.

Tap the "Generate answer" button for a response from the AI.

This project requires access to your camera and audio. Please tap "Accept" to continue.
